The Healthier Option

For the first time it is possible to produce red grape juice that contain the same benefits found in red wines as well as other beneficial flavonoid found in red wine.

At Inah Superior Products we pride ourselves on being the pioneers in producing a grape juice with the complexity of wine. We strive to produce a range of prestigious juices that reaches out to wine drinkers and non-wine drinkers alike, by taking the benefits of wine and making a juice that reaches out to all people, irrespective of medical conditions or religious convictions.

Through our unique natural extractions process, we have released all the nutrition found in wine grapes into Inah giving it not only the complexity of flavours found in wine, but also its health benefits in a juice that contains absolutely no alcohol.

Inah offers an alternative to wine. It is prestigious in taste, as it is in appearance being the perfect addition to any dinner table. Inah superior Grape Juice is made from 100% pure cultivar grapes. No sugar is added. The sweetness is naturally obtained from the glucose and fructose contained naturally in the grape.
