What We Do

From your doorstep in Singapore to the consignee doorstep in Jakarta, in just 8 hours

Specialized services are available for Aircraft On Ground parts (AOG), ship’s spares in transit, valuables and personal effects. This performance is achievable by constantly upgrading our capabilities using technology and a network of customs brokers to avoid delays in cargo clearance.

Door to Door Service

Our main product is our swift and efficient Door-To-Door (DTD) services that ensure your shipment will always reach on time.

Speed is our greatest forte when it comes to sending your shipment to your clients. If time is against you, we have our top notch express services and also Hand-Carry and Rush Handling services to further speed up the delivery process.

Experts in Sea, Land and Air freight

With 15-20 years of experience in the logistics and supply chain industry, our services consist of both air and sea channels with movements via containers, full, part loads and airway unit load devices.

We also provide land transportation to neighbouring countries and various connecting international destinations or for domestic purposes. Our prompt, efficient and transparent customer relation service will further increase your company’s branding and reputation towards your clients.

International network

When working with DC Freight Solutions, you can expect a transparent relationship as our motto is to treat our clients like partners.
